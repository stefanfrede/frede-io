Curriculum vitae

Hi there, I am Stefan. I am a freelance front-end developer from Germany based in Hamburg. Because I am travelling while working, I can be found all over Europe.

I have been working in this business for over 15 years now and I am specialised in the conception and creation of functional, content sensitive web pages. My approach is to look ahead, not into the past. That's why I develop web pages with the rich feature set of modern browsers in mind while making sure that older browsers with a more limited feature set still display the website as best as possible.

For the rare occasions that I am not in front of my computer, I spend my time hiking and running.
